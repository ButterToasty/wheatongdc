ABOUT THE CLUB

The Game Development Club is a club where people with creativity and skills will have the opportunity to create video games

Activities

Our club's activities consist of designing and creating playable video games using programs such as Unity, Unreal Engine, and Godot

Passion

Our motivation as a club is creating video games for people to enjoy while applying our personal skills to make it happen

Meetings

We will always have weekly meetings on Thursdays in room 3712 but if we must meet more than once a week then we will contact the club's members

Learning

Anybody can join even if you don't have any skills in game development. This is also a learning experience if you are interested in programming and art
